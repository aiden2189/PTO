The Band

ABOUT PTO

We're not your average rock band — we're a father and son sharing the stage and a love for music.

Led by dynamic frontman Scott Theis ("Scotty") and his son Ayden ("Slim") on drums, we deliver a high-energy mix of originals and covers that hit hard and keep the crowd moving. Scotty & Slim trade off on drums and vocals, and every member sings, so no two sets are ever the same.

TWO GENERATIONS. ONE STAGE. ALL ROCK.

Rounding out the lineup are Ethan Schluter ("Hank") and Mikey Delp ("Cowboy") on guitars, with Jakob Ripp ("JR") holding down the low end on bass — together, we create a live show that brings rock lovers of all ages together.

Nashville Recording Trip

This February, PTO headed to Nashville and spent long days at Stoner Creek Studios — owned by Peter Keys of Lynyrd Skynyrd — recording our upcoming 5-song EP.

Produced by Malcolm Springer, the EP also features killer keyboard tracks from Jakob's dad, Jim Ripp. We had visits from Peter Keys, Chris & Jane Dobry (Stryker Records), Scotty Austin, and the Major Label Entertainment crew.

Between Gibson Guitars, Third Man Records, vintage stores, Broadway, house jams, and a fierce game of Candyland — it was unforgettable.
